Faq's

You can expect to be waiting anywhere from a couple of weeks to a couple of months. Although we may have space sooner it’s advised to contact us at least a month before you’d like to get tattooed, however this also depends on the artist as some have longer waiting lists. We cannot accommodate walk ins or same day appointment unless stated otherwise (we will usually post last minute spaces on social media).

We generally price by the piece and will give you a quote based on the size, placement, style and complexity ofyour idea. Our minimum charge is £50 and this is reserved for very small, simple single-colour designs. A day session is £400.

We currently do not accept card payments and have no future plans to do so. Please bring cash to your appointment.

All tattoo appointments require a deposit upon booking, the space cannot be held without it. It’s then deducted from the final payment on the day of your tattoo. All deposits are non refundable but can be transferred, provided you give us at least 48 hours notice before your appointment.
